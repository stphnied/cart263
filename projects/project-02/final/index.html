HOW TO PLAY

YOU ARE NOW

You are an independent cute cat that loves to collect miniature toys from games. You usually spend your day leisuring around and napping, but your favorite game shop came out with new toy machines and you NEED to complete your collection.


COLLECTION

You can access your current collection at all time by clicking the COLLECTION button. You can also see all the toys that you are missing to complete your collection.v Each machines carry various item.


EARN COINS

At the start, you are given 5$ to spend to your liking. Once you are out of money, you can take on a freelance job on the Job board. Each job will give you a different depending on your performance.

How to play:
Click and drag a coin into the coin-slot to activate the claw-machine.
If you want a refund -> click on the button next to the coin-slot
click and hold onto the joystick to move the claw.
Press the button to drop down the blaw.

** I have yet implement a prize drop
** You cannot ask for a refund once you move the joystick
